NUWE organizes the Mobile World Congress hackathon for the second consecutive year

As part of the Mobile World Congress, NUWE was present at 4 Years From Now (4YFN), a space for innovators, creators and leaders to come together and showcase their potential to the most influential executives in the technology sector. And it did it doubly: with its standwhere it received more than 50 companies interested in the platform and a considerable number of developers, and as co-organizer of the Hackathon promoted by Mobile WorldCapital as part of the Barcelona Digital Talent initiative.

The International FemHack: the programming event that breaks the “algorithm” of gender inequality in the technology sector

On January 21, 22 and 23rd, 2022, more than 1,500 women from more than 50 countries (including Spain, the United States, Australia and Peru) participated in the online event The International FemHack: the first female-only Hackathon, accompanied by educational conferences presented by women leaders from the technology sector. The event was managed and coordinated by NUWE from Aticco Workspaces's installations in Barcelona.
